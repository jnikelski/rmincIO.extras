The following references must be included in any publication
using CIVET.

CIVET Pipeline
==============
  Y. Ad-Dab'bagh et al., "The CIVET image-processing environment: A
  fully automated comprehensive pipeline for anatomical neuroimaging
  research", in "Proceedings of the 12th Annual Meeting of the
  Organization for Human Brain Mapping", M. Corbetta, ed. (Florence,
  Italy, NeuroImage), 2006

Non-uniformity corrections
==========================
  J.G. Sled, A.P. Zijdenbos and A.C. Evans, "A non-parametric method
  for automatic correction of intensity non-uniformity in MRI data",
  in "IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging", vol. 17, n. 1,
  pp. 87-97, 1998

Stereotaxic registration
========================
  [1] D. L. Collins, P. Neelin, T. M. Peters and A. C. Evans,
  "Automatic 3D Inter-Subject Registration of MR Volumetric Data in
  Standardized Talairach Space," Journal of Computer Assisted
  Tomography, 18(2) pp. 192-205, 1994

  If avg305 model is used, cite:
  ------------------------------
    Stereotaxic registration of the MRI volumes was achieved using the
    MNI305 target [2].
    [2] A. C. Evans, D. L. Collins, S. R. Mills, E. D. Brown, R. L.
    Kelly, and T. M. Peters, "3D statistical neuroanatomical models from
    305 MRI volumes," San Francisco, CA, USA, 1994.

  If icbm152 linear model is used, cite:
  --------------------------------------
    Stereotaxic registration of the MRI volumes was achieved using the
    ICBM152 linear target [3].
    [3] J. Mazziotta, A. Toga, A. Evans, P. Fox, J. Lancaster, K. Zilles,
    R. Woods, T. Paus, G. Simpson, B. Pike, C. Holmes, L. Collins,
    P. Thompson, D. MacDonald, M. Iacoboni, T. Schormann, K. Amunts,
    N. Palomero-Gallagher, S. Geyer, L. Parsons, K. Narr, N. Kabani,
    G. Le Goualher, D. Boomsma, T. Cannon, R. Kawashima, and B. Mazoyer,
    "A probabilistic atlas and reference system for the human
    brain: International Consortium for Brain Mapping (ICBM),"
    Philos Trans R Soc Lond B Biol Sci, vol. 356, pp. 1293-322, 2001.

  If icbm152 non-linear 6th generation symmetric model is used, cite:
  -------------------------------------------------------------------
    Stereotaxic registration of the MRI volumes was achieved using the
    ICBM152 non-linear 6th generation target [4].
    [4] G. Grabner, A. L. Janke, M. M. Budge, D. Smith, J. Pruessner,
    and D. L. Collins, "Symmetric atlasing and model based segmentation:
    an application to the hippocampus in older adults," Med Image Comput
    Comput Assist Interv Int Conf Med Image Comput Comput Assist Interv,
    vol. 9, pp. 58-66, 2006.
